About

Dongju Kim

Hi, I am a robotics engineer with a strong interest in legged robot learning. I am currently a post-master researcher at CAMEL Lab. I earned my M.S. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering and my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Pusan National University (PNU). During my M.S. studies, I had the opportunity to work under the guidance of Prof. Inho Lee, conducting research on reinforcement learning for legged robots, with a focus on both blind and vision-based locomotion.

Current research interests

  • Learning-Based Robot Control and Sim-to-Real Transfer
  • Dynamic Legged Locomotion
